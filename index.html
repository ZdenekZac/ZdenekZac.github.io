"In 2018, my journey into web development began during a course on Application Development Fundamentals at E-College, Dublin. Despite initial setbacks in landing a junior web developer role, I gained valuable experience as a transport manager and system-app admin in a logistics company. Managing daily plans felt akin to coding — juggling variables and ensuring everything aligns — sparking my renewed passion for programming.

This realization prompted me to pursue formal education at Tamwood College in Vancouver, where I completed a Web Development Professional course. Post-graduation, I embarked on a self - driven learning path through Udemy Academy, mastering HTML5, CSS3, SASS, modern JS, React, WebGL, Python, Arduino C++, and more.

Driven by an unwavering commitment to continual growth and mastering new technologies, I am poised to apply my skills as a junior web developer, coder, or programmer."
